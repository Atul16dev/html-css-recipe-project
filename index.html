Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup maida (all-purpose flour)
  • 1.5 cups sugar
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 1.5 cups water
  • ~1/4 cup warm milk
  • Ghee/oil for frying
    •

    Recipe

    1. Syrup: Boil sugar, water, cardamom for 8 mins. Add rose water + lemon juice.
    2. Dough: Mix dry ingredients, add ghee + milk. Make soft dough, rest 10 min.
    3. Shape: Roll small crack-free balls.
    4. Fry: Deep fry on low heat until golden brown.
    5. Soak: Add fried balls to warm syrup. Let soak 2 hrs.

    Kaju Katli (Cashew Barfi)

    Ingredients

  • 1 cup cashew nuts (kaju) powdered
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 tsp ghee (for greasing)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder (optional)
  • Edible silver leaf (vark) (optional)
    •

    Recipe

    1. Grind 1 cup cashews into a fine powder without releasing oil.
    2. Grind 1 cup cashews into a fine powder without releasing oil.
    3. Add the cashew powder to the syrup and cook on low flame for 5 to 7 minutes, stirring continuously until it forms a soft dough.
    4. Let the mixture cool slightly, then knead it gently using 1 tsp ghee until smooth.
    5. Roll the dough between parchment paper to 1/4 inch thickness.
    6. Apply edible silver leaf (optional) and cut into diamond shapes.
    7. Cool completely and store in an airtight container.
